# Leafy greens & lettuce

Leave bagged greens in their original (ethylene-permeable) packaging. Wrap loose heads in moist paper towels inside a partially open bag in the crisper. Keep greens **away from apples and bananas** — those ethylene producers wilt them fast (see `_ethylene`).
