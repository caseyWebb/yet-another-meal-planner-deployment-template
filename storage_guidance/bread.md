# Bread (never the fridge)

Counter or a bread box for the next few days; freezer for anything longer. The fridge is the **worst** spot — cold actually stales bread about twice as fast. To freeze, wrap in foil inside a zip-top bag (~1 month) and refresh in a hot (450°F) oven. Same goes for cakes, cookies, and muffins.
