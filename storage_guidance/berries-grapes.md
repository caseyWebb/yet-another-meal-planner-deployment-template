# Berries & grapes

Don't wash them until you're about to eat them — surface moisture is what breeds mold. Store grapes **on the stem**, unrinsed; de-stemming opens entry points for bacteria.

(Some cooks rinse berries on purchase in a water-vinegar solution to kill surface mold spores — the mechanism is real, but results vary and the "lasts three weeks" claims are oversold. Treat it as optional, not a rule.)
