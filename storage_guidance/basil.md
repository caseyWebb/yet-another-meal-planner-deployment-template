# Basil (the counter exception)

Stand it stem-down in a glass of water at **room temperature**, like cut flowers. Basil is the one tender herb that does better on the counter than in the fridge.

(The room-temp advice is solid; the often-repeated "the cold *blackens* it" explanation is folk-reasoning, not something the test kitchens actually demonstrate — so the placement is the real tip, not the mechanism.)
