# Fresh meat & poultry

Keep the fridge at 35–40°F and store raw proteins **well-wrapped on the bottom shelves**, so nothing drips onto ready-to-eat food. Whole cuts keep 3–5 days; ground meat 1–2 days — otherwise freeze. If you're stacking, order them top to bottom by safe-cook temp: whole cuts higher, ground meat and poultry at the very bottom.
