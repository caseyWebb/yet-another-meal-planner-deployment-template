# Potatoes & winter squash

Potatoes, sweet potatoes, and winter squash all want a **cool, dark** spot away from light (light turns potatoes green). Do **not** refrigerate potatoes — cold converts their starch to sugar and throws off the flavor. Keep potatoes **away from onions** (see `_ethylene`).
