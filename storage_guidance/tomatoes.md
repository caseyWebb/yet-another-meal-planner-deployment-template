# Tomatoes

Unripe → leave on the counter to ripen. Dead-ripe → the counter still tastes best, but the front of the fridge buys you a few days (bring them back to room temp before eating). Cold mutes the flavor and turns the texture mealy.

(There's a real split here: ATK leans "counter, period"; Serious Eats' taste tests allow the fridge once fully ripe. The safe rule: **unripe → counter; dead-ripe → either.**)
