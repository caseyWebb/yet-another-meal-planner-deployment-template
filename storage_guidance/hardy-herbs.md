# Hardy (woody) herbs

Rosemary, thyme, sage, oregano.

Wrap in a paper towel, slip into a zip-top bag, and keep in the crisper. Tough, woody leaves keep better a little drier than tender herbs do. (Sources differ slightly on damp vs. dry towel — ATK leans damp, Serious Eats dry — but either beats leaving them loose.)
