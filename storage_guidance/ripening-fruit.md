# Ripening fruit (counter to ripen)

Bananas, peaches, nectarines, plums, apricots, mangos, papayas — ripen them on the counter, out of direct sun. The fridge stalls ripening and leaves them mealy. Move them to the fridge only **once ripe**, to hold them a few extra days.

To speed things up, bag an unripe one with a banana or apple (see `_ethylene` — same gas, on purpose).
