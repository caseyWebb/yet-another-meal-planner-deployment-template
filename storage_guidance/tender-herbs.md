# Tender herbs

Cilantro, parsley, dill, tarragon, mint, chives.

Stand the stems in ~1 inch of water, loosely covered with a bag, **in the refrigerator** — or wrap them in damp paper towels in a zip-top bag in the crisper. They wilt fast and want moisture, but keep the *leaves* out of the water or they rot. (Basil is the exception — it goes on the counter, not the fridge.)
